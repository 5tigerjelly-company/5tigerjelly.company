Simply Screenshot - Privacy Policy

Our screenshot extension must have a privacy policy because it collects user data, specifically the screenshots captured by users, their annotations, and related metadata. The privacy policy ensures that users are informed about what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected. This transparency builds trust and ensures compliance with legal requirements.

Data Collection

We collect screenshots, annotations, titles, and timestamps. For premium users, we may also collect data related to cloud storage and sharing.

Data Usage

The collected data is used solely to provide and improve the extension's functionality. Screenshots and related data are stored locally within the browser, and optionally, in cloud storage for premium users.

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

User Control

Users have control over their data, including the ability to delete screenshots and related metadata.

Third-Party Services

If applicable, we disclose the involvement of third-party services, such as cloud storage providers, and how they handle user data.
